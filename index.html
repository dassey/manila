🛬 Getting to the Hotel (Fri night, ~20:05 arrival)
Option 1 — Walk the Skybridge (10 min, preferred)
  1. After deplaning at SEA, clear U.S. Customs & Immigration (international arrival from Manila).
  2. Collect checked bags at Baggage Claim.
  3. Follow signs for Link Light Rail / Parking Garage — this leads to the pedestrian skybridge.
  4. Cross the skybridge over International Blvd. The hotel is immediately on the other side.
  5. ✅ At 20:05 landing, you're well within skybridge hours (open until 12:01 AM). Plenty of buffer.
Option 2 — Free Hotel Shuttle (backup, 24/7)
  1. After baggage claim, follow signs for Ground Transportation / Courtesy Vehicles.
  2. Go to Island 1 or Island 3 at the courtesy vehicle pickup.
  3. Pick up the courtesy phone and dial "28" (or ask operator for SkyBridge Hotel).
  4. Shuttle runs every 20–30 min, ride is ~5 minutes.
🛫 Getting Back to SEA (Sat morning for 07:45 flight)
  1. Target: be at SEA check-in by 5:15 AM for a 7:45 AM domestic departure (2.5-hour buffer with kids + TSA).
  2. Leave hotel by ~5:00 AM. Skybridge reopens at 4:00 AM, or use the shuttle (24/7, on-demand between midnight-4 AM — call the front desk first to schedule pickup).
  3. Set two alarms — phone alarm + hotel wake-up call. Request a 4:00 AM wake-up at check-in Friday night.
  4. Pack the night before so kids can roll straight out of bed into clothes.
💡 Practical tips for this stay Hotel check-in is 3:00 PM, check-out 11:00 AM — your ~20:30 arrival is fine. Grab dinner at Reflections Grill on-site or the hotel marketplace for a quick bite. Kids will be jet-lagged hard (coming off a 12+ hour flight across the Pacific) — straight to bed is probably the move.
